    * `flexGrow`
    * `flexShrink`
    * `flexWrap`
    * `justifyContent`
    * `vectorEffect`
* Fixed typos in `borderRightStyle` and `lineHeight`.
* Fixed warnings in `ReactCssTransitionGroup`. ([#86](https://github.com/japgolly/scalajs-react/issues/86))
